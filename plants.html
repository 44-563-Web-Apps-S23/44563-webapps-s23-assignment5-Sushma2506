Plant's name row breaking first as grid classes apply to devices with screen widths greater than or equal to the breakpoint sizes, and override grid classes targeted at smaller devices. Therefore, e.g. applying any .col-md-* class to an element will not only affect its styling on medium devices but also on large devices. In col-md -> md meant for medium screens and col-sm --> sm meant for smaller screen so this is why plant names got broke.s
